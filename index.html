Our Programs

Male Allies Program

"Men and women, building solutions together.” A platform that engages men as champions of gender equality. This program equips male allies with knowledge, empathy, and tools to understand challenges faced by women and to take part in creating lasting solutions.

POISE TALKS

“Conversations that shape confidence and purpose.” A series of personal development sessions for young women, featuring mentorship, skills-building, and inspiring stories. Poise Talks equips participants with practical tools for confidence, resilience, and leadership.

NEXT GEN LEADERS

“Igniting the future, one student at a time.” A mentorship initiative for high school students. Through career guidance, character-building, and role model sessions, Next Gen Leaders nurtures future changemakers who dare to dream big and lead with courage.

CAMPUS TOWN HALLS

“Real voices. Real issues. Real solutions.” An open forum for university and college women to discuss pressing challenges, share experiences, and co-create solutions. These interactive town halls foster sisterhood, advocacy, and collective action for safer and more supportive campus spaces.
