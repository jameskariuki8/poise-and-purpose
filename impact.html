Our Journey

Poise & Purpose Kenya began in 2024 with a simple but bold conviction: to raise a generation of grounded, confident and purpose-driven young women.

What started as small gatherings and conversations has grown into a movement shaping leadership, identity and impact across Kenya.

2024 — The Beginning

We laid the foundation for a community that empowers young women to grow with clarity, confidence and character.

January 2025 — Our First Leadership Training Conference

In partnership with Kisii University, we hosted our inaugural leadership training conference, reaching over 500 young women.


This milestone cemented our vision to build spaces where young women can learn, lead and thrive.

May 2025 — Launch of the Male Allies Program

Understanding that gender equity requires partnership, we launched the Male Allies Program.


Since then, over 100 young men have joined us in supporting the mission—standing for equality, mutual respect and a redefined culture of leadership.

August 2025 — Gen Next Mentorship Program

As part of our sustainability pillar, we introduced the Gen Next Mentorship Program for teenage girls.


This initiative ensures younger girls grow up equipped with mindset, values and mentorship that prepares them for purposeful adulthood.

And We Are Still Becoming…

Our journey is still unfolding, grounded in purpose and driven by a growing community of women (and allies) who believe in building a better, bolder and more intentional generation.
