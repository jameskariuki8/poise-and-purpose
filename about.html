🌸 Our Story

Poise and Purpose Kenya began in September 2024, in Kisii County, with a dream bigger than ourselves to see young women and girls rise as bold, confident leaders.

  1. Sep 2024 · Kisii County

    A dream bigger than ourselves

    We began with a bold vision: to see young women and girls rise as confident leaders — shaping their communities with courage and purpose.

  2. Conversations → Circles

    From talk to togetherness

    What started as a few conversations in classrooms and community spaces quickly grew into circles of mentorship, safe spaces for dialogue, and campaigns challenging the norms that hold girls back.

  3. Urgency & Hope

    Dismantling barriers, daring to hope

    We were born out of urgency and hope: urgency to dismantle patriarchy, and hope for a Kenya where leadership is not limited by gender.

  4. Across Kenya

    From Kisii to the nation

    From Kisii to the rest of the country, our movement carries one message: every girl has power, every girl has purpose, and every girl can lead.

“Every girl has power. Every girl has purpose. Every girl can lead.”
